Frequently Asked Questions

A: All data is stored only on your iPhone/iPad. No data is sent to external servers.

A: Currently, data can be transferred through iCloud backup. More convenient migration features will be added in future updates.
Q: Can I use it on iPad?
A: Yes, you can use it on iPad. The app supports both iPhone and iPad.
